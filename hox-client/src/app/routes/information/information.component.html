General Information

Welcome to the World Cup 2018 trading application!

The trading is modelled as financial forwards, where each participating country has a) an (non-tradable) Index instrument valued according to the team's World Cup results, and b) a (tradable) Derivative (on the Index) that is valued by the market.

The Index instrument contains historic values (Closing prices) from previous tournaments, and at the end of World Cup 2018, each index will be updated with a new value. When this is done, the Derivative trades will be settled using the Closing price of the underlying instrument.

The Closing prices are calculated as follows:

  • 1:st 30
  • 2:nd 25
  • 3:rd 20
  • 4:th 15
  • Quarter-finals 10
  • Round of 16 5
  • Group Stage 0

E.g. in World Cup 2014 England was knocked out in the Group Stage, and thus got 0 points. Costa Rica did an impressive effort and got al the way to the quarter-finals and was therefore awarded 10 points. And the winner, Germany got the highest score of 30 points!

From the scoreboard above it can thus be derived, that (at the end of the tournament) no team will be worth less than 0, and no more than 30. So, if you believe that Germany will repeat the success from laast time, you would want to buy the Germany Jul 18 Derivative for a price less than 30 in order to make a profit when (if) the deal settles at 30 at the end of the tournament.

E.g. Buy 10 Germany Jul 18 for 25 SEK each. If settling @ 30 SEK, (i.e. at settlement you sell back at 30 SEK), you have made a profit of (30-25) * 10 = 50 SEK.
OBSERVE! that if Germany only ends up in 4:th place, you will instead lose on the deal: (15-25) * 10 = -100 SEK.

What if you on the other hand believe that Germany won't be able to perform as good as last time, and at best reach the Quarter-finals? Then you should take a short position (i.e. sell first and buy back at settlement) in Germany Jul 18 at a price higher than 10, and cross you fingers that they fail miserably.

E.g. Sell 50 Germany Jul 18 for 16 SEK each. What happens? Germany is kicked out in the Round of 16 and only gets 5 points. You have made a profit according to (16-5) * 50 = 550 SEK.
OBSERVE! that if Germany ends up in 3'rd place (or higher) you will make a loss.
FAQ
Why do the derivative names end with Jul 18?

The derivatives are automatically named according to the commodity derivatives convention of using the underlying name plus the month and year of expiry. Since World Cup ends in July 2018, the name becomes e.g. Germany Jul 18.

Why is the expiry date July 16:th, when the final is on the 15:th?

The applocation has a builtin restriction that only allows trading up until the day before settlement. But in order to keep betting alive during the final the expiry has been set to the day after. So! When the final is over, make sure that you don't have any active orders left in the market, or you may be hit badly.
Actually this is relevant throughout the whole tournament. If e.g. England is kicked out in the group stage, we will then know that their settlement price will be 0. But since all derivatives expire on July 16:th, it will technically be possible to continue trading in England. You should therefore make sure that you don't have any outstanding orders in teams that has been kicked out of the tournament, or you may be hit badly.

How do I trade?

Tading is made on an open market where you are free to enter Buy and Sell orders according you your market belief. If a Buy Order matches with a Sell Order, this will result in a Trade. There is no central clearing of trades, so you will get a counterparty for each trade that is made. This is the counterparty(s) that you will later on do the settlements with.

How will the settlement be handled?

Settlement processing is automated, and will be triggered as soon as a settlement price is available. This means that settlements can be done throughout the tournament as soon as a team gets kicked out. When a settlement instruction has been created it will appear under the Settlements section.
Since trading is settled peer-to-peer, you will get one settlement instruction per counterparty. If you are the payer, you will get a QR code for Swishing the settlement amount to you counterparty.
Don't forget to acknowledge when you have swished the settlement amount, and when you have recieved the settlement amount.
